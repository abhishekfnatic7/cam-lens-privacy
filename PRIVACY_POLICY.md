# Privacy Policy for RTSP Player

**Last Updated:** March 28, 2026

## Introduction

RTSP Player ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information.

## Information Collection

### What We Do NOT Collect

RTSP Player is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT:

- Collect personal information
- Track your location
- Access your contacts or phone data
- Store your RTSP stream URLs on our servers
- Share any data with third parties
- Use advertising trackers

### What We May Collect (With Firebase Analytics)

If Firebase Analytics is enabled, we may collect anonymous usage data including:

- App crashes and error reports (via Crashlytics)
- General usage statistics (app opens, feature usage)
- Device type and Android version
- Country/region (non-precise)

This data is used solely to improve app stability and user experience.

## Data Storage

### Local Data Only

All RTSP stream URLs and NVR configurations are stored locally on your device. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not backed up to any cloud service
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

### Snapshots

Screenshots taken within the app are saved to your device's local storage (Download/RTSPPlayer folder). These files remain on your device and are not uploaded anywhere.

## Network Access

The app requires internet access to:

- Connect to RTSP streams on your network
- Connect to NVR devices you configure

All network connections are initiated by you and connect only to servers you specify.

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Internet | Connect to RTSP streams |
| Network State | Check network availability |
| Wake Lock | Keep screen on during playback |
| Storage | Save snapshots to device |

## Children's Privacy

RTSP Player is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact the developer.
